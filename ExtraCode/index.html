NEW FEATURE  July 13, 2022
Introducing Record Types

Does your business involve dealing with different categories of contacts, accounts, and deals? B2B contacts, B2C contacts, enterprise accounts, mid-market accounts—there are just so many types. Unless you've found your own way to differentiate all of these records, it can be a bit of a daze.

To make life easier, record types are here. Once your admin configures the different record types your business needs, use them to categorize your contacts, accounts, deals, and and even custom modules. You can also set up advanced automations based on record types.
